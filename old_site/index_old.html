Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC) advised by Prof. José Luis Ambite and Prof. Yao-Yi Chiang. Before that, I received my Master degree in Data Informatics Program from USC in 2017 and my Bachelor degree in Information Security from Tongji University in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Zhijun Ding.

More information is available in my CV and on our Spatial Computing Lab website.


Research Interests

  • Data Mining - Discovering interesting patterns from large-scale datasets
  • Spatiotemporal Data Analysis - Learning spatial and temporal relationships in the location-dependent timeseries data
  • Machine Learning - Building ML/DL models to automatically extract useful information to solve real world problems
