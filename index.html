About Me

I am working with Prof. Yao-Yi Chiang in the Knowledge Computing Lab at UMN CS&E.

I was in the University of Southern California (USC) advised by Prof. José Luis Ambite and Prof. Yao-Yi Chiang.

My research focuses on spatiotemporal data analysis with machine learning. I am interested in incorporating prior knowledge about ''SPACE'' in the data-driven methods to solve complex real-world problems. Here, the ''SPACE'' can be the geographic space on earth or the represention space learned from data.
