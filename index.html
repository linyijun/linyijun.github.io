About Me

I am working with Prof. Yao-Yi Chiang in the Knowledge Computing Lab at UMN CS&E.

I was in the University of Southern California (USC) advised by Prof. Yao-Yi Chiang and Prof. José Luis Ambite.

My research focuses on spatiotemporal prediction and forecasting. I am interested in incorporating prior knowledge about the ''SPACE'' in data-driven methods to understand environments and human behaviors. The ''SPACE'' can be the geographic space on earth or the representation space learned from data.
