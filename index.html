Biography


I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC) advised by Prof. Yao-Yi Chiang and Prof. Craig Knoblock. Before that, I received my Master degree in Data Informatics from USC in 2017 and my Bachelor degree in Information Security from Tongji University, Shanghai in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Zhijun Ding.

My research interests are data mining, machine learning, pattern recognition, and spatial science. I have been working at Spatial Computing Lab for two years. I am mainly involved in building algorithms and systems that relate to spatial data mining and pattern recognition with large-scale data. More information is available in my curriculum vitae.
